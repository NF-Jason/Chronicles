# Former Goddess of Art and Song
- Created by the [[Goddess of Life]] and [[The One]]
- Once married to the [[God of Murder|First God of the Forge]] 
- Was raped by [[First God of War]]
- bore two children the [[Goddess of War and Strategy]] and the [[God of Passion and Desire]]
- Committed suicide and let herself fall from the Celestial Plain, past the mortal realm, past the under-spires deep into the abyss.

# After the Fall
- She returns with the help of [[The Banished One]] as the first Infernal Goddess
	- At the moment of unexsistance, deep in the abyss, there [[The Banished One]] could interact with the first part of existents, otherwise anathema to it. And he imbued her with new life and new purpose as first of the Demons, Queen of the Dammed.
- She brought madness into the world of Mortals
