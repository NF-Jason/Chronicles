She is the Great Spirit of Negentropy, also known as the World Tree. As a deity, she is considered a great spirit and a mate to [[The One]] supreme god. It is believed that by honoring and paying homage to her, individuals can ensure a long and peaceful life, as well as the well-being of their communities.

Overall, she is an important figure in the pantheon of gods and plays a crucial role in maintaining balance and stability in the world. Devotees of her often participate in rituals and ceremonies to honor the deity. Offerings of fruits, flowers, and other natural elements are given to the World Tree, symbolizing the importance of nature and the interconnectedness of all living things. The tree itself is also a symbol of strength and resilience, as it can withstand the test of time and adapt to changing circumstances.

In some myths, it is said that she can communicate with other Great Spirits and even [[The One]] supreme god. Its wisdom and insight make it a valuable source of guidance and inspiration for those seeking to understand the mysteries of the universe.

Overall, her role as the Great Spirit of Negentropy makes it a crucial component of the world's mythology and culture. Its presence is a constant reminder of the importance of balance and harmony in all aspects of life.
