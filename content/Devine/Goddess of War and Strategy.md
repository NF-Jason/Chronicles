Child of the violence of the [[First God of War]]  and madness [[Demon Queen of Madness]] (Former Goddess of Art and Song), she solders the burden, the weight of what War really means. Twin sister to the [[God of Passion and Desire]]
- Discipline and a keen mind hold in the instincts of a brutal warrior
- Born with a natural affinity for music, which she uses to inspire her followers
- Protector of the wronged and patron of orphans.
- Associated with mead, a drink that brings strength and courage to the drinker.
