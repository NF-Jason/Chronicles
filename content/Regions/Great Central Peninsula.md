# During the Ages
## Time of Legends
This land was home to the [[God of the oceans]], his godly decedents and may tribes of his demi god children (all daughters).

## The First Age
The tribes that remained after the fall still call these lands there home. The many tribes have spread, and are still faithful to the old ways and the dream joining with there father. They are still a very strong pure demigod bloodline in this age.
